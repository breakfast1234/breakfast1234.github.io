The Game's Inspiration

The game is inspired by Fire Emblem, a grid-based, turn-based strategy game. Dimension 87 similar but also features multiplayer.

Mechanics

Both players pick a team of four fighters. Both players take turns moving their units and attacking the other player's units. Different units have different attack ranges, abilities, and other stats. The first player to lose all their fighters loses.
